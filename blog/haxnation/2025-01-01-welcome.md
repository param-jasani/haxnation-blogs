# Welcome to the Haxnation Blog

We're thrilled to launch the **Haxnation community blog** — a space for insights, research, tutorials, and stories from our growing community.

<!-- truncate -->


## How to Contribute

Every blog post goes through a review process to maintain quality and security. Here's the flow:

1. **Fork** this repository
2. Create your post under `blog/<your-github-username>/`
3. Submit a **Pull Request** to `main`
4. Our automated checks run (authorization + content isolation)
5. A blog-reviewer approves and merges your post

Check out the [Contributing Guide](https://github.com/haxnation/blog/blob/main/CONTRIBUTING.md) for full details.
